Who We Are

Altarsound 360° is your one-stop-shop multimedia services provider with interests & expertise spanning across audio, visual, music production, digital & print media.
With over two decades of operation including more than a decade of successful trading in the UK media circuit under our belt, undergirding what we do are years of professional training & experience in diverse climes; global best practices in service delivery; and a penchant for going over & above in our pursuit of excellence in every project.

What We Do

 

 
Altarsound Studios

Altarsound Studios is a cutting edge, flagship project recording and rehearsal facility in the heart of Calabar that is equipped with all that your music & media ideas need to be brought to life.

We are endowed with experienced and highly skilled producers, session musicians and recording engineers, guaranteeing you an unparalleled rehearsal or recording experience.

Our bespoke recording/rehearsal studios are properly soundproofed, acoustically treated, well-lit, homely, spacious, fully air-conditioned and versatile enough to accommodate a live studio set-up, live recordings with or without audience, as well as rehearsal sessions and video/photo shoots. Our location is superb. We enjoy fantastic transport access: the popular, ever-bustling Marian Road and IBB Way are both only two minutes walk in either direction of our facilities. Our location has free parking, free WI-FI, 24-hr security and guaranteed power supply.

Our services include: Rehearsals |Music Production | Equipment Rental | Multi-track Studio & Location Live Recording | Mix & Mastering | Audio Post-Production | Sound Design, Sound/Music to Picture & Voice-overs for Radio/TV | Commercials, Documentaries, Montages |Sermon Editing/ Restoration | Video Production | Graphic Design |Disc Print, Duplication & Replication | Multi-Cam Live Streaming Solutions

Altarsound Worship Resources

The identity and name ‘Altarsound’ is a merger of two words; 'altar' & 'sound; it symbolizes our understanding of our calling in CHRiSt Jesus that beyond just making praise and worship rise from some perceived altar, we are that sound; that living sacrifice, that altar. We are the worship we bring, flowing forth as the spirit and sound from the depth of our hearts—His altar!

We are a Worship Resources Development Outfit with a simple, crystal-clear mandate: “Redefining Worship On God's Terms!”

We go about this in a number of ways: our worship development seminars; our Worship Development School; our ‘Worship Discovery Series’ books & articles; our flagship events including ‘TheoGnosis’, ‘The MORE Sessions’ and ‘OASIS—Your Place Of Refreshing’; and our label imprint ‘Altarsound Music’.

A great deal of our calling and passion revolves around teaching & instructing in the ministry, lifestyle & art of worship to raise worshipers with deep and clear understanding. We develop our own teaching material by the revelation of the Holy Spirit and a culmination of God’s dealings in our lives and many years of ministry experience.

 

 
PAV & Altarsound

The worship team arm of the ministry, known as ‘PAV & Altarsound’, have been blessed and privileged to lead worship extensively across the Europe & Africa. No other vocation or profession gives us as much fulfillment, and the praise reports of lives transformed by the knowledge of CHRiSt Jesus and who they are in Him as captured and expressed in our songs unleashing tangible manifestations of God’s Spirit & presence make it all worthwhile!

The team is made up of a family of regular, everyday people who are crazy about God. From University students, full-time ministers and sales personnel to professional musicians, media/graphics designers and IT geeks, all of us are united with one purpose: to be worship to the MOST HIGH! Together we form a warm, loving, fiercely united, passionate and simple bunch of no-nonsense, crazy about God and absolute all-the-way worshipers—actually, worship-smiths— crafting the hearts of men back to the place of true worship of the MOST HIGH as CHRiSt is being formed in them.

As God opens the doors, we are keen to share fellowship in worship with the Body of CHRiSt around the world. We go where He sends us: Churches, concerts, crusades, conferences, TV appearances, tours, support, etc.
